Washtenaw County Meals on Wheels & Senior Cafe Program

The Washtenaw County Meals on Wheels & Senior Cafe Program (www.washtenaw.org/snp) is coordinated by the Washtenaw County Office of Community and Economic Development (OCED). OCED is committed to stepping out of traditional government roles to drive long-term system changes that increase equity and opportunity.
